Purpose

The main purpose of charity is to provide support to people in need, including providing food, water, health care, education and other basic needs. Philanthropy also aims to create opportunities for the development and success of people in need, by providing education, vocational training, financial assistance or other services that help them become independent and independent go ahead further in life. Participating in charitable activities helps build an empathetic and supportive community in which people share and care for each other, creating a positive and peaceful living environment.
