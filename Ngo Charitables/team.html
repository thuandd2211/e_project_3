FOUNDER

Dr. Mayrose Salvador

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Dr. Mayrose Salvador is a Filipino-Canadian scientist, innovator, and educator. She received her B.Sc. in Chemistry from the University of the Philippines and a PhD in Physical Chemistry from the University of Toronto. She completed postdoctoral degrees at the University of Minnesota and the University of Toronto. As a scientist, Dr. Salvador created advanced materials for solar cell applications, pioneered the use of new laser techniques to create industrial applications for nanomaterials, and contributed understanding of the impact of these materials on the environment. She holds patents and has co-authored 15 scientific publications that have been cited more than 480 times.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Dr. Cynthia Goh is Professor of Chemistry and Institute of Health Sciences and Director of the Impact Center at the University of Toronto. He received his B.Sc. in Chemistry from the University of the Philippines and a Ph.D. from the University of California, Los Angeles, is researching experiments and theory on phase transitions and critical phenomena. He did postdoctoral research at Columbia University in the area of nonlinear optics and at the University of California, Berkeley in solid state vibrational spectroscopy. Professor Goh is a strong advocate of the concept of excellent basic science, which forms the basis for new technology and leads to wealth creation. Dr. Goh is active in global health issues, especially related to medical diagnosis for under-resourced settings.
